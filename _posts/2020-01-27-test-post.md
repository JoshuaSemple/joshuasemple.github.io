*First Test Post*

<!--break-->

The rain fell heavily, the muffled thumps echoing throughout his apartment. He’d been meaning to move for a while now, but he’d grown comfortable and as such was procrastinating with the moving process. Alistair sat, lounging upon his aged lazy boy. Archimedes, his cat, lay on his lap, sprawled across his legs, clearly delighting in the warmth of the nearby fireplace. “It’s the simple things, isn’t it Arch?”, he sighed, closing his eyes momentarily, taking comfort in the presence of the little cat. He must have dozed off, for when he opened his eyes, the fire’s glow had diminished and rain was nothing more than a gentle patter. A yawn of disgust erupted from Archimedes as he lazily stretched, disgruntled by Alistair’s movement. “I’m sorry buddy”, he smiled, gently stroking the kitten in a futile attempt to placate the now grumpy feline. There was a sudden knocking sound. “Mr Alistair?”, a soft spoken high pitched voice could faintly be heard from the front door. Alistair frowned, glancing at his watch. _Eleven pm? No good comes from being disturbed this late_. He grunted and stood, placing the kitten closer to the fire. “I’m on my way”, he called, groggily making his way to the door.
